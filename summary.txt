Hi Team,

Thank you everyone for the valuable discussion and feedback on the proposed AI-Driven Operational Intelligence & Monitoring solution for DMO.

Below is the summary of key discussion points and agreed next steps:

• The proposed solution will enhance the existing DMO ecosystem without replacing current monitoring tools, alerts, workflows, or integrations.

• Existing operational data sources including Azure Monitor, Log Analytics, alerts, pipeline metadata, SLA information, run history, dependency mappings, and operational documents will be leveraged as inputs for AI-driven insights.

• The proposed approach includes implementing a RAG-based knowledge retrieval layer using Azure Functions/Logic Apps, Python-based document processing, embeddings, Azure AI Search, and Meta IQ integration.

• Feedback was received to keep the initial POC focused and manageable by starting with selected pipelines/use cases and following an MVP-based implementation approach.

• Additional technical detailing is required for:

* SLA early warning implementation
* Real-time vs scheduled monitoring strategy
* Integration/data flow design
* Cost estimation
* Governance and compliance considerations

• It was recommended to:

* Change “Benefits” to “Expected Benefits”
* Reduce overcommitment during the POC stage
* Focus on 3–4 measurable and achievable outcomes

• Additional guidance was provided to:

* Include Microsoft official documentation within the knowledge repository strategy
* Ensure architecture and messaging consistency across teams
* Coordinate with global stakeholders for alignment and scalability considerations

Next Steps:

* Consolidate all feedback into the proposal and presentation
* Refine POC scope and prioritization
* Prepare detailed implementation and governance inputs
* Estimate infrastructure and operational costs
* Schedule follow-up technical deep-dive discussions

Thank you again for all the inputs and support. I will consolidate the feedback and continue refining the proposal accordingly.
